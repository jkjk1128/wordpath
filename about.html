About Word Hop

What is Word Hop?

Word Hop is a daily word puzzle game that challenges your vocabulary and problem-solving skills. Each day brings a new puzzle where you connect two words by changing one letter at a time.

How to Play

The goal is simple but engaging:

  • Start with the first word (e.g., COLD)
  • Change exactly one letter at a time to create valid English words
  • Continue until you reach the target word (e.g., WARM)
  • Complete the puzzle in as few steps as possible

Example path: COLD → CORD → CARD → WARD → WARM

Features

  • Daily Puzzles: A new challenge every day at midnight
  • Multiple Solutions: Many puzzles have several valid paths
  • Three Attempts: You get three tries to find a solution
  • No Registration: Play instantly without creating an account

Purpose

Word Hop is designed for entertainment and educational purposes. It's a fun way to expand your vocabulary, practice spelling, and exercise your brain with daily word puzzles. Whether you're a word game enthusiast or just looking for a quick mental challenge, Word Hop offers engaging gameplay for all skill levels.
